ABOUT US
History
Founded in 2010, Bark International has a rich history of crafting exceptional fragrances that capture the essence of luxury. From its humble beginnings as a small boutique fragrance house, Bark International has evolved into a global brand renowned for its exquisite perfumes. Over the years, the brand has stayed true to its commitment to using only the finest ingredients and techniques to create unique and long-lasting scents that stand out from the crowd. Today, Bark International continues to innovate and inspire, creating fragrances that evoke a sense of sophistication and elegance, making it a go-to choice for those who seek the finest aromas.
Our Mission
At Bark International, our mission is to craft exquisite fragrances that inspire and captivate. We believe that perfume is not just a scent, but a form of expression that evokes emotions, memories, and feelings. As such, we are committed to using only the finest ingredients and techniques to create unique, long-lasting fragrances that stand the test of time. Our goal is to create perfumes that become a part of the wearer's identity and elevate their everyday experiences. We are dedicated to sustainability, social responsibility, and ethical practices, ensuring that our fragrances are not only luxurious but also environmentally friendly. At Bark International, we are driven by a passion for quality and an unwavering commitment to excellence, and we strive to create scents that leave a lasting impression and inspire the world.
Our Values
At Bark International, our values are at the core of everything we do. We are committed to creating exceptional fragrances that not only smell beautiful but also reflect our dedication to sustainability, social responsibility, and ethical practices. We believe in using only the finest ingredients and techniques to create unique, long-lasting scents that evoke a sense of sophistication and elegance. We are passionate about innovation and creativity, always pushing the boundaries to create new and exciting fragrances. We value transparency, honesty, and integrity in our relationships with our customers, suppliers, and employees. We are committed to supporting the community and giving back through our partnerships with various charitable organizations. At Bark International, we believe that fragrance is an art form, and we are committed to creating beautiful scents that inspire, captivate, and enrich the lives of our customers.
